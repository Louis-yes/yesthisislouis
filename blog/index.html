I'm working on a few cards at the moment, I really need to get a printer.

This first one here is an idea I had about approaching moments in life in which one feels multiple, sometimes conflicting but strong emotions. I want to do cards that aren't so comfortable, even just to experiment. I want this to be an artform, not because I see 'relatable' cards as below me but because I think they can be both, and perhaps doing one will help me with the other.

With this second one I'm trying to copy Andres Tena's illustration style, because my own is lacking. This wouldn't be a commercial one, just for practicing.
