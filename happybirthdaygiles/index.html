### Happy Birthday Brother. ###

I can believe it has been a year since we last made these, so much has happened between then and now. I'm sorry we haven't seen each other more often, but you probably more than most understand why I'm so far away and what vague dreams I'm chasing. Making this podcast is special because it really captures a lot of the spirit of those dreams, as well as pulling them into the warm light of day.

Of course this isn't about me, except that the nature of this gift is to bring forth what is within me, for you.

You have a had a huge year, and I'm extremely proud of all you've achieved. Our calls have not been as frequent, because some of what you've achieved is alive and demanding. This is true in part for many things we make, but true in entirety for Fenn and Alistair.

This episode was a joy, once again, to make. What a great gift, a gift that gives to all. I was going to wax lyrical about how much you inspire me, but I've read last year's birthday card and it looks like I've covered that. Not much has changed, but my admiration and gratitude have grown. Thank you so much for your support, encouragement, and care.
